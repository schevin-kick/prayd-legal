Prayd

Support & Help

Prayd is a Christian prayer community where you can share prayer requests, intercede for others, and celebrate answered prayers. This page is here to help.

Contact us

Questions, bug reports, or want to report inappropriate content or a user? We're glad to help and aim to respond promptly.

Email: hello.prayd@outlook.com

Reporting objectionable content or abuse

Prayd has zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive users. You can act immediately from inside the app:

  • Report content: open any prayer or comment, tap the menu (•••), and choose Report.
  • Block a user: from a prayer or comment menu, choose Block — their content disappears from your feed.
  • Manage blocks: Settings → Blocked accounts.

We review reports and act on objectionable content within 24 hours by removing it and ejecting the offending user. You can also email hello.prayd@outlook.com to report anything.

Deleting your account or data

You can delete everything yourself, in the app, with no email or extra steps: Settings → Account & data → Delete. Your account and all associated content are removed immediately.

Notifications & location

Push notifications and location are optional and can be turned on or off at any time in Settings (and in your device's system settings). Your precise location is never shown to other users.

Age requirement

Prayd is for adults only. You must be 18 or older to use the app.

FAQ

  • Is Prayd free? Yes — always. Optional one-time gifts are entirely voluntary.
  • Can I post anonymously? Yes, you can post prayer requests anonymously.
  • I forgot my password. Use "Sign In" → reset, or email us and we'll help.
